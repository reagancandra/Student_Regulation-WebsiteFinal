STUDENTS REGULATIONS CHAPTER 2

Chapter 2: Students Behavior Standards

1. Elyon Christian School upholdsits vision,mission, and values as the foundation of its school culture.

Vision: Elyon becomes a progressive leader,is continually committed to progress and known by its Christian values, moral, ethics and skills as well as its ability to thrive in the midst of change in Indonesia.

Mission: To establish formal Christian Education which has global perspectives and maintains Christian values with strong holistic education in Indonesia.


Fruit of the Spirits:

  • Love
  • Joy
  • Peace
  • Patience
  • Kindness
  • Goodness
  • Faithfulness
  • Gentleness
  • Self-control

Regulations:

2.1. Virus Prevention Measures

  1. Wear a mask when feeling unwell
  2. Sneeze into your arm or elbow
  3. Always bring your own stationery, food, utensils, tumbler, mask, and hand sanitizer

2.2. Greetings

  1. Practice smiling, greeting, and showing respect to everyone in the school community
  2. Greet your teachers at the beginning and end of each lesson

2.3. Communication

  1. Communicate using proper English
  2. Show respect to the person speaking
  3. Request permission before asking questions in class

2.4. Responsibility

  1. Arrive early and attend all classes according to the schedule
  2. Wear the complete school uniform neatly and properly on school grounds
  3. Maintain personal and environmental cleanliness
  4. Take care of personal belongings and use school facilities appropriately
  5. Submit all assignments on time
  6. Respect the culture of queuing
  7. Maintain order during school events

2.5. Permissions

  1. Use a hall pass when going to the restroom
  2. Use a hall pass and fill in the off-classroom form when leaving the class during learning hours
  3. Complete the off-campus form when requesting to leave school during school hours

2.6. Examinations

  1. Students are required to take all exams according to the scheduled time
  2. Students must comply with all exam regulations

  3. Students who miss exams without valid reasons will receive a maximum score of the Minimum Mastery Criteria (KKM), unless they are absent due to illness (with a doctors note) or bereavement

  • 2.7. Worship Time

    1. Students must respect worship time by giving full attention during the session
    2. Students are required to take notes during sermons or devotions and submit them to their mentor
    3. Students are not allowed to chat, study, or do homework during worship
    4. Students are not allowed to disturb others during worship
    5. Students must bring a printed Bible when attending worship

    3. In line with the vision, mission, and values of Elyon Christian School, we expect every student to reach their full potential and apply the behavioral standards outlined above.
